Background

The Provincial Water Quality Monitoring Network is a program that provides data on the quality of stream water networks across Ontario and covers a wide range of parameters. The current interface provided for displaying the data is designed to show active versus inactive stations on a map and give spatial context to people exploring its accompanying open source datasets but it does not go further than that. The data collected through this program has the potential to play a role in engaging the general public with water quality and protection issues. The lack of a platform that clearly displays collected data in a way that is accessible for the general public alongside more information about the importance of water quality is a problem. The data that comes from programs like these is invaluable and the continued funding of existing programs as well as funding being allocated to new programs can come through public pressure on legislation.

Goal:

To create an Esri Dashboard to display the collected data along with a summarization of important water quality parameters. Alongside this Dashboard will be an Esri StoryMap to communicate the reason the program exists as well as general information about water quality monitoring programs
